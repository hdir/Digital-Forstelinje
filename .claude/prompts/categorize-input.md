Can you try to categorize the inputs by what kind of stakeholder gave the input?

## Input

Each stakeholder have given input contained in a single file in the directory: background\helsereform\markdown

## categories of stakeholders

We are most interested in these categories of stakeholder
* Virksomheter i helsesektoren - organizations delivering health care in the norwegian health care sector
* Direktorater og andre offentlige virksomheter - organizations that is involved in the public sector but is not delivering health care, organizations like helsedirektoratet, FHI etc.
* Undervisning og forskning - organizations doing science, schools and universities
* Helsepersonell - health care personell or organizations representing health care personell groups like legeforeningen, kiropraktorforeningen, etc
  * there are a number of individuals in this category as well.
* Ideelle organisasjoner - ideal organizations typically organizing public intrests like patient organizations.
* Kommuner og kommunesektor - municipality, KS and fylker falls in this category
